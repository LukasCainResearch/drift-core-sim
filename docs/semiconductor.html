1. Overview

What it solves:

  • Non-deterministic reset behavior and undefined startup states
  • Silent state corruption under clock, voltage, or logic fault
  • Loss of liveness guarantees in hardware pipelines
  • Inability to distinguish delay vs failure at silicon speed

Interpretation note: demonstrations show observable invariants (timing, state transitions, reset enforcement), not RTL disclosure.
