1. Overview

What it solves:

  • Replay and stale-message acceptance under stress
  • On-path tamper detection without heavy protocol overhead
  • Jamming-induced delay classification (TIMEOUT vs compromise)
  • Mesh continuity with quorum and recovery policy

How to read the demos: they demonstrate protocol invariants (freshness, timing, quorum) using tutorial math, not the proprietary construction.

DECISION INVARIANTS

Fresh challenge + strict deadline window + expected/received comparison → PASS/FAIL/TIMEOUT. Quorum policy controls degrade/recover behavior.
