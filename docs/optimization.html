2. Applications in Industry

Logistics Routing

Optimizing the "Last Mile" for delivery fleets is a classic TSP. Even a 1% reduction in total route distance saves millions in fuel. Drift Annealing finds these 1% gains by exploring route combinations that standard heuristics discard.

Chip Floorplanning (VLSI)

Arranging transistors to minimize wire length is computationally expensive. We are exploring the use of Drift IP Cores to run background annealing cycles during the physical design phase.
