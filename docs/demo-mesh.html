Challenge/Response Mesh

Tutorial abstraction showing freshness (replay rejection), timing budget (deadline/TIMEOUT), and quorum recovery (>=2 PASS) as a minimal trust mesh.

What this is: a narrated visualization of protocol invariants.
What this is not: a cryptographic implementation or a disclosure of proprietary construction.

How to interpret outcomes

  • FAIL → content mismatch (tamper, replay, wrong state/key)
  • TIMEOUT → exceeded timing budget (delay/jamming classification)
  • CONSENSUS → quorum governs degrade/recover policy (>=2 PASS)
