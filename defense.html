OPERATIONAL CAPABILITIES

Built for Anti-Access/Area Denial (A2/AD) environments.

01 // SWARM-SYNC

GPS-Denied Navigation

When GPS is jammed, swarms lose coherence. DOC-MIL provides a monotonically increasing arithmetic heartbeat. Drones synchronize maneuvers based on the "Drift Sequence Index" rather than external time, maintaining formation in full spectrum denial.

02 // DRIFT-HOP

Un-Jammable Comms (FHSS)

Standard PRNGs for frequency hopping are linear or slow. Drift Core generates non-linear FHSS sequences in <5 nanoseconds. This provides "Look-through" capability faster than enemy jammer reaction times.

03 // KINETIC IFF

Zero-Latency Identification

Hyper-velocity interceptors have microseconds to identify a target. AES handshakes are too slow. DOC-MIL enables a 1-Cycle Arithmetic Challenge-Response, securing the kill chain without latency penalties.

04 // SMART DUST

The Physics Limit

You cannot fit AES on a sensor the size of a grain of rice. By utilizing pure affine logic (Shift + Add), DOC-MIL scales down to ~200 gates (Serialized Mode), enabling crypto on printed electronics and shell casings.
